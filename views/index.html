    Currently TV, radio, newspapers and internet are widely used to spread information about missing people. The idea behind "Little Brother To The Rescue" is to utilize mobile devices to improve search activities. With the app installed on their smartphones people will get notification about missing person only if they are occasionally came into search area. So, if many people have this app on their phones, all of them will be notified and will be able to help with rescue. Basically all they need is to take a look around and check if missing person is near them. Very simple action being done by many people in right place and right time can help to find one missing person.
